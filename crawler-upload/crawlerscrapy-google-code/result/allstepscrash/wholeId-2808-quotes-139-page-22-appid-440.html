Ok, thanks for the feedback.

There is indeed some known problem with custom ROM (based on cyanogen audio drivers) in the version on google play.

The nightly build version basically disable one setting (which is also available in expert audio troubleshooting section of the playstore version, but was enabled by default). That's why normally fixed in nightly builds and will be included in next release.
