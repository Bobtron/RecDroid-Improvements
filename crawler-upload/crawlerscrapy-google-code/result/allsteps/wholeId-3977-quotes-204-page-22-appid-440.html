csipsimple - issue #803

Keep alive in 3G (mobile): no call possible in/out even if marked as registered

Posted on Mar 14, 2011 by Helpful Bear

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.in Wifi mode all working fine 2.as soon we go to Mobile (3G), the registration work fine, I can start call and I can recieve call. 3.But after a couple of minute, no more calls are possible.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? That I can continue to recieve call ans start call as the case in Wifi mode. Instead, after a couple of minute de phone seems to be disconnected from the SIP server.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? The latest on the market, on Android 2.2.1 on HTC Desire Z

Please provide any additional information below. I have changed the 'Keep alive mobile' parametre down to 10s but without success. I tryed also to put the 'registration time' parameter down to 15s but again without success.

It work very fine in Wifi.... bug in 3G mode or configuration problem?
