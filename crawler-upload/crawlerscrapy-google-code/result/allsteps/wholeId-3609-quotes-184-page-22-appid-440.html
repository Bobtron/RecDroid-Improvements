Can't Initiate SRTP_enabled and TLS_enabled Call

Posted on Aug 26, 2011 by Happy Lion

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Enabled SRTP and TLS in CSipSimple client(phone) ,Blink client(PC) and Asterisk

Server.

  1. Initiated a call from Blink to CSipSimple . CSipSimple accepted,call established.

  2. Initiated a call from CSipSimple to Blink. Blink didn't receive call message,didn't

ring. Can't terminated this call in CSipSimple(push 'hangup' button). I recorded the

sip flow in Asterisk, file in attachment.

  1. Enabled SRTP and TLS in two CSipSimple phones,let them called each other. The

appearance is the same as step3.

5.Enabled SRTP and Disabled TLS in CSipSimple client and Asterisk,Enabled SRTP and TLS

in Blink client(PC) . CSipSimple and Blink can called each other,both established

successfully.

6.Disabled SRTP and Enabled TLS in CSipSimple client,Enabled SRTP and TLS in Blink

client(PC) . CSipSimple and Blink can called each other,both established successfully.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

in step 3: the Blink should receive the call and ring,but blink did't.CSipSimple should

terminated the call right now when i pushed 'hangup' button, but it didn't ,and it

automatically released this call after about 30s.

int step 4：the call should be ringing,but it didn't. And the call Initiating

CSipSimple can't terminated the call.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? TLS_r1014, android2.2.1 . Asterisk 1.8.5.0 on ubuntu.

Please provide any additional information below. It seems that TLS and SRTP can't work together.
