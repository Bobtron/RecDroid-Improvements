csipsimple - issue #217

NeufTalk Unable to cancel a call, with provider "NeufTalk"

Posted on Sep 17, 2010 by Grumpy Ox

  • What steps will reproduce the problem?

    1. Register to NeufTalk (sip.wengo.fr)
    2. Make a call (Status "Calling" > "Rigging")
    3. Try to cancel the call, before the other side answer the call

  • What is the expected output? The call is supposed to be canceled (Status "Rigging" > "Hung Up")

  • What do you see instead? Instead, I have to wait for the other side to answer.

  • What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? I'm using 0.00-12-32 on an HTC Desire 2.2

  • Please provide any additional information below.

  • When I use NeufTalk as trunk for Pbxes.org, and setup my Pbxes account in csipsimple, everything is working fine !

  • I have capture the IP traffic with tcpdump, and csipsimple DOES send the CANCEL request.

  • I have tried with an other SIP application found on the Market (LinPhone), and the result is the same ...

  • I can provided you the logs and capture files by email if you want.

I submit the problem here, just to know if you see something strange in the logs ... but NeufTalk may be the cause of all this :p

PS : Thaaanks a lot for your GREAT application, it's already the best SIP application for Android, even if it's still in alpha version
