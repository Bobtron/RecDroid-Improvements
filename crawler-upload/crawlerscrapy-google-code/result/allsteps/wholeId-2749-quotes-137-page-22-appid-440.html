about med_cnt in vid_set_android_renderer

Posted on Nov 27, 2012 by Grumpy Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.make video call 5 times 2. 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? render view not displayed.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? i am using r2006.

Please provide any additional information below.

when i make video calls for 5 times(call id is from 3 returned to 0),render view is not displayed. in pjsua_jni_addons.c, function vid_set_android_renderer: when ready to call vid_set_stream_window, because med_cnt is 1, and only contain audio info, the stream of video is not seted. so, render view cannot be displayed. i think med_cnt and media information is not correctly seted when call id is from 3 returned to 0.

i donot know how to fixed it.

i change PJSUA_MAX_CALLS to 32 to avoid this bug. no one make 32 video calls continuely. but, the bug is still there.
