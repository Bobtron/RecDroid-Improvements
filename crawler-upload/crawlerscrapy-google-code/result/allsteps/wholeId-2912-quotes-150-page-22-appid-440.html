csipsimple - issue #1936

no I/O audio on HTX One X unless earpiece plugged in

Posted on Sep 5, 2012 by Quick Cat

What steps will reproduce the problem? - place any call from HTC One X (I did VoIP to VoIP using wifi)

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? - no sound heard on HTC One X, the other party does hear sound well (so mic works) - no sound via loudspeaker either (pressed speaker icon) - when I plug in earphones, sound is perfect and loudspeaker works too

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Android 4.0.4 HTC Sense 4.1 SW 2.17.401.2

Other comments: Sometimes freezes in the call & the app then needs to be killed externally. In that case, to place another call, I need to reboot the device, otherwise nothing happens after dialing the #. Happened multiple times when I played with the above.

Comment #4

Posted on Sep 10, 2012 by Quick Cat

Ok, so I played around with three "outputs": 1) internal 'close-up' speaker (default) 2) internal loudspeaker 3) external earpiece with mic (shipped with the phone)

Observations: - a general prerequisite for a good sound is to set the microphone boost to about 12dB, otherwise very quiet audio at the other end - speaker boost is fine at 0dB - with the above, earpiece and close-up give perfect sound on both sides - loudspeaker sounds fine, but the other party has pretty low output in that case - I disabled 'echo cancellation' (or whatever it's called in En, I have it in Czech) because in no conditions it was helping - the 'audio focus' option has no effect in all conditions - note that until I realized I can use HW volume buttons, I tried to boost volume with SW buttons in the app which gave overdriven-like yet still quiet sound. Resetting to 0dB & using HW buttons obviously solved it. - finally, in-car bluetooth does not work, app hangs & reboot necessary. Should I open another thread?
