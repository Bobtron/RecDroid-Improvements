csipsimple - issue #1419

No In/Out Audio on Amaze 4G, when I check Mode Audio API' & Toggle 'Phone/Speaker' 4-5 times, works. WTF?

Posted on Dec 8, 2011 by Grumpy Rabbit

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Install CSimpleSip on Amaze 4G 2. Add an account 3. Try to make a call 4. No audio (both ways). Nothing received on other end. Nothing heard. Other phone hears nothing. 5. Enable Mode Audio Option 6. Press the Speaker button a few times, first few times you'll hear 'clicks' of the audio, then after a few more (5-6), the audio works on the speaker, and also works on the phone itself.

Packets are sent. Problem occurs both with PCM/G729. Server connects and transmits packets fine. VOIP account works fine with other SIP clients. Incoming/Outgoing calls are received and made fine, no problems, just no audio until you press the Speaker button 5 times.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Audio is supposed to work.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? HTC Amaze 4G AWS Band (WIND Mobile in Canada) Android 2.3.4 HTC Sense 3.0 Software 1.45.1500.2 Kernel 2.6.35.13-g52e85af htc-kernel@u18000-build-144 #1

Please provide any additional information below.
