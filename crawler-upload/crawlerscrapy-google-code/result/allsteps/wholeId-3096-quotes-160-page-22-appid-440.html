Comment #1

Posted on May 5, 2012 by Quick Hippo

Most of the time sip providers doesn't allow to add contry code with a "+". They often requires users to replace it with 00 for example (this can depend on the sip provider, but most of the case it's 00).

So, you need to find the good replacement for "+". Once done, don't be afraid, you'll not have to replace that in all your contacts :). CSipSimple has one advanced feature "Fitlering/Rewriting rules" that allow you to automatically rewrite phone numbers that comes from external apps. See http://code.google.com/p/csipsimple/wiki/UsingFilters
