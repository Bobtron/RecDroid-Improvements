Comment #2

Posted on Oct 20, 2011 by Grumpy Wombat

If you are oc/uv'ing, try without any oc/uv and see if the problem happens then. 99% of the time when theres freezing/rebooting, its because of unstable OC/UV settings. Especially if your using the same setting from a different kernel (as Talon uses different frequencies and voltages than other kernels. Also, you may have to adjust your OC/UV settings depending on the governor, as the governor will change how the cpu scales, ...ie..one governor might use lower frequencies more, putting less load on the higher frequencies, so you might be able to get away with more UV..and vice versa with other governors that might use higher frequencies more. But just try without OC/UV, if it does not happen then, then its unstable settings..

Also, starting with 1.0.7, the kernel will limit the max frequency to 652mhz when the phone enters suspend mode.. which unstable oc/uv settings can very easily trigger sleep death..etc..
