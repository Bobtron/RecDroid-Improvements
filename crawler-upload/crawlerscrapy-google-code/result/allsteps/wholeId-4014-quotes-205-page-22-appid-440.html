Posted on Mar 5, 2011 by Quick Hippo

All settings you need for sipgate are available in the sipgate help center. (In your personalized sipgate help center).

You can read this : http://www.sipgate.com/faq/article/397/How_do_I_set_up_my_VoIP_device

Go on sipgate website, login and : * Click on My Settings. * Place your cursor over the phone and then click on SIP Credentials. * All the information you need to enter is displayed in the following box.

Sipgate wizard does not automatically set the sip server cause sipgate use several sip server according to your country. Be also careful abou the fact SIP-ID is different from your login ! But in sipgate wizard it should be crystal clear if you have their help interface + the csipsimple SipGate wizard under your eyes. Fields names are identical ;).
