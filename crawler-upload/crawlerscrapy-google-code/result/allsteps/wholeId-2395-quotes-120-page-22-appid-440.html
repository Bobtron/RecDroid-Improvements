csipsimple - issue #2416

Native and Skype diallers unavailable when CSipSimple is active

Posted on Jun 27, 2013 by Grumpy Bird

What steps will reproduce the problem?

  1. Integration of diallers accepted
  2. No dialling rules
  3. Access CSipSimple app and tap SIP account(s) icon

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Two (2) registered SIP accounts appear. The account for 'mobile' is missing.

Dial a non-SIP number and it is attempted via a SIP account (no 'chooser' appears). 'disconnect' CSipSimple and the 'chooser' with Phone and Skype appear.

N.B. Dialling rules have no effect ('can't call' + 'is exactly' + number) in SIP accounts. CSipSimple uses a SIP account.

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system? CSipSimple 1.00.00 r2225 GT-S5830 / CM10.1 RC6 Skype 3.2.0.6673

Please provide any additional information below.
