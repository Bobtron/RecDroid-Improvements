csipsimple - issue #321

v15-06 broke back "hard" button (Samsung Vibrant).

Posted on Oct 29, 2010 by Swift Hippo

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Install v0.00-15-06dev build 2. Launch CSipSimple 3. Press back button on Samsung Vibrant in dialer (nothing happens)

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Should revert to previous window (e.g. text dialer<->digit dialer or desktop, whichever was last) Was working in v15 stable.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Latest dev build, 15-06. OS: ECLAIR.UVJI6 (2.1-update1 stock). Samsung Vibrant T959 phone.

Please provide any additional information below. Back button works to get out of settings menus but does nothing in the dialer menus. Have to press 'home' hard button to minimize application whereas previously 'back' worked too. Doesn't erase partially entered phone number.
