csipsimple - issue #2904

On kitkat 4.4.2 if process is closed the app is offline. - No incomming calls

Posted on Mar 8, 2015 by Grumpy Hippo

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Press and hold "home". 2.Pull (left or right)the icon "csipsimple" to close it. 3.On kitkat 4.4.2 is close the process, the service is still open. 4.Now phone is offline.

In other apps (for example Viber) the service can run his process for incoming calls.

But the csipsimple can't run his process during incomming calls and the phone can't receive the calls.

Version csipsimple 1.02.03.

OS kitkat 4.4.2
