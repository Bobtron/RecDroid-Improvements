csipsimple - issue #1345

SAS isn't displayed on the screen even if the conversation is secured (ZRTP-UI related issue)

Posted on Oct 25, 2011 by Helpful Lion

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Register on a Freeswitch server (which runs in proxy media mode) with 2 Android clients. 2.Enable ZRTP on both clients (turning on Create ZRTP from Secure Transport menu) and make a call. 3.Answer the call

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The expected output: The conversation is secure and the 4 digit SAS is displayed in the top right corner on both clients. What do I see instead: The SAS isn't displayed at all on any of the phones ( most frequent) or is just displayed on only one of them.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? I am using CSipSimple r1062. HTC Desire HD (Android 2.3.3) and 2 Samsung Galaxy Mini (Android 2.2.1) clients connected to a Freeswitch server (which runs in proxy media mode).

Please provide any additional information below.

The problem is that I only sometimes see the 4 digit SAS displayed on both phones,sometimes the SAS appears only on one phone but most often it is not displayed at all on any phone, although ZRTP is active (I know that because I registered on the Freeswitch server with a client on the phone and a client on a computer (Jitsi-former SIP Communicator) and in Jitsi it showed me that ZRTP is active and the conversation is secured, but on the phone it didn't display the 4 digit SAS or that ZRTP was active).

The catch is that if I select "Disconect" from the app menu, and I restart the app again (on both phones) then, on the first call made after that the SAS is displayed on both phones, but on the other calls it is just like I've mentioned before. So, even if ZRTP is active, the user won't know that because it isn't displayed anywhere and he can't verify the SAS with the other party.
