csipsimple - issue #778

Galaxy S Bluetooth/speakerphone noise and deadlock

Posted on Mar 8, 2011 by Massive Camel

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. start a call 2. press "speaker" and/or "bluetooth" button 3. repeat quickly 100 times step 2

What is the expected output?

Change 101 times audio route.

What do you see instead?

99 times correct behaviour, once 5 second horrible noise (the same sound happens to me in our program on Galaxy S when I called setSpeakerphoneOn(true) when bluetooth is on) and deadlock at the end.

What version of the product are you using?

0.001-01 r615

On what operating system?

Android 2.2, Samsung Galaxy S

Please provide any additional information below.

I am a programmer of Acrobits Softphone for Android, I have troubles with Galaxy's specific audio issues too and I wanted to test my competitor :-) Good luck.
