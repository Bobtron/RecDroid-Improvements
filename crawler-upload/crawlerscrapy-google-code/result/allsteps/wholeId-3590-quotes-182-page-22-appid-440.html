csipsimple - issue #1216

No audio on Sprint Nexus S 4G 0.02-03 (any rev)

Posted on Aug 17, 2011 by Happy Dog

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Place call 2. No audio in or out 3. PSTN terminated or VOIP terminated

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Bidirectional audio

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.02-03 r1002 (and earlier) Samsung Nexus S 4G (Sprint) Cyanogen 7 Nightly 79 Netarchy Kernel 1.3.9

Please provide any additional information below. I could not get CSipSimple to produce any in call audio whatsoever. DTMF tones during dialling were present. I tried every option related to routing with the Advanced option enabled. (routing API, Mode audio API, Samsung Galaxy Hack, Tone hack, Focus audio) I attempted changing Audio mode for SIP calls as well, no change. All indications both on the SIP server and on-screen (background goes 'green' and call timer starts at same time as call supervises). Changes in codec did not make a difference. (Tried ulaw/PCMU and speex)

Works correctly on a sister Nexus S GSM on T-Mobile with an identical config except for account name/password (however with previously reported non-functioning speakerphone issue). This GSM unit also runs CM7 nightly and the same Netarchy kernel.
