What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Make a call

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The sound has frequent popping noise.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? HERO CDMA with CSipSimple 0.00-15

Please provide any additional information below. The log was flooded with the following: W/AudioFlinger( 205): RecordThread: buffer overflow
