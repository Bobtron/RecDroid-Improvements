Comment #3

Posted on Dec 8, 2010 by Quick Hippo

@dmitry : in your logs I see one successful connection (maybe on wifi?).

Then it tries on 3G network, CSipSimple correctly send requests. However it get no response from the server. So response is 408 timeout - I think that's different from the initial issue with bad gateway.

In this case most of the time (here in france it is the case) it's cause 3G provider block SIP traffic. Are you sure that your 3G carrier allow VoIP SIP on their network? (I mean SIP protocol not a proprietary VoIP protocol such as Skype).

However, there is other case where it can occurs. I get an interesting log at the beggining of your logs about STUN : it seems that it's a double NATed environment which is really complicated to solve for SIP calls. It was working previously with 0.00-15 on 3g network for you too?
