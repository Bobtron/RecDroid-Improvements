csipsimple - issue #1957

Microphone noise in 0.04 and nightlies, not in 0.03

Posted on Sep 12, 2012 by Swift Rabbit

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Make or receive call / perform audio test 2. Overwhelming outgoing noise when using v0.04-01 and all nightlies ~r1560-r1891, but not in v0.03-01 3. Audio hacks, codecs do not solve in v0.04-01, but work in 0.03-01

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Significant outgoing noise is heard in v0.04-01 and all current nightlies, but v0.03-01 is clean when Tone Hack enabled (Focus Audio, Samsung Galaxy hack don't make a big difference).

In v0.04-01, I have tried many combinations of WebRTC implementation on/off, routing+mode APIs on/off, Samsung Galaxy Hack, Tone hack, Focus audio, Micro source (default/mic/communication), audio mode (normal/communication), java/open-sl es, etc., but all combinations have significant microphone noise and anything I actually say sounds very quiet/distant.

Is the change in behavior between 0.03-01 and the current releases due to something internal to CSipSimple or is it from a new version of an external dependency? I would be happy to test any new code or send logs if you can recommend something to try.

The settings I'm using in v0.03-01 are: WebRTC: off Routing API: off Mode API: off Samsung Galaxy Hack: on Tone hack: on Focus audio: on Micro source: VOICE_COMMUNICATION Audio mode for SIP calls: IN_COMMUNICATION

Again, toggling the Samsung Galaxy Hack and Focus audio don't seem to matter; the Tone Hack is the one that makes a difference.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Phone: Samsung Galaxy SL (GT-I9003) OS: Gingerbread 2.3.6

The changes in pjsip is a good idea. But regarding changes there is only two candidates that could be root cause of that, and I don't think it's the problem.

However, on my parts there is a more significant change that could be the root cause. In r1524 the audio layer setup is made before audio stream creation. In r1525, it's made before audio start (and after audio stream creation). For example in this file : http://code.google.com/p/csipsimple/source/diff?spec=svn1525&r=1525&format=side&path=/trunk/CSipSimple/jni/pjsip/android_sources/pjmedia/src/pjmedia-audiodev/android_jni_dev.cpp
