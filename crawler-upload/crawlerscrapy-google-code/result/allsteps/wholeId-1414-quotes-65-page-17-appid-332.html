Posted on Oct 13, 2009 by Happy Bear

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Plug in usb cable that is connected to the computer. 2. Wait for the phone to suspend itself. 3. Press the power button once to wake it up from suspend

What is the expected output? The phone should wake up properly.

What do you see instead? The phone completely locks up with led in AUX button blinking.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? android-on-freerunner-cupcake-0.1.1.tar.gz
