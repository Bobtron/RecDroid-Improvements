csipsimple - issue #2276

CSipSimple fails to re-register PBXes account after dropping the connection

Posted on Mar 7, 2013 by Helpful Rabbit

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Boot Phone w/CSipSimple as the active SIP client using PBXes.org account. 2. Let time pass (sometimes hours, sometimes minutes) 3. OR Clear Ram / Active Applications

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? CSipSimple should reregister the SIP automatically. Instead, CSipSimple doesn't reregister the PBXes.org account. When you go to the settings, you see PBXes.org in red with a message "Error while registering - Unauthorized". In order to get it to reregister, I have to either shut down CCsipSimple and restart it or turn the PBXes.org account off and then on again (switch it from Red status to Grey Status and then press it again to activate it to Green status).

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.04-05 r2154 on Jelly Bean 4.1.2

Please provide any additional information below. This is a serious problem that I've reported multiple times. I've captured CSipSimple logs where this has happened and have submitted them. I never know when it disconnects unless I look at it, so I am missing calls left and right when this happens. I wish that someone would just make adjustments to the code that when it throws this error to automatically do that action sequence to get it to register properly (turn off the account then turn it back on). I can't attach a screenshot here (on this site) because it says "Issue attachment storage quota exceeded", however I sent in screenshots with the logs.

Comment #7

Posted on Oct 18, 2013 by Happy Hippo

I running the most up-to-date version from the Play Store, for my device.

To get to the setting, in CSipSimple:

1) Open your list of SIP accounts (the key-list icon at the bottom of the screen) 2) Enable expert mode by long pressing your PBXes SIP account, and select "Choose wizard'. Pick the "Expert" wizard near the bottom of the list. 3) Open your PBXes SIP account settings by tapping it. 4) Enable "Send empty auth header" option in the "SIP AUTHENTICATION" section.
