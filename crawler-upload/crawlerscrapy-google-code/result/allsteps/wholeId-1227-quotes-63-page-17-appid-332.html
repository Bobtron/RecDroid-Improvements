android-on-freerunner - issue #72

Battery indicator suddenly drops

Posted on Nov 10, 2009 by Grumpy Camel

What steps will reproduce the problem? Can't tell for sure, but I noticed this behavior while browsing on WiFi, GSM not connected (the SIM card in the Freerunner has expired), GPS off, Auto screen rotation (accelerometers) off, brightness @ 30%, ... Anyway, sometimes the battery indicator says there suddenly is much less battery available (I've noticed drops from 80% to ~20%, from ~60% to ~10% and even had a forced shutdown with no way to abort it when the battery was in fact at around 50-60% !) and it goes back to normal minutes later without any obvious reason, even if I continue to browse via WiFi... See http://groups.google.com/group/android-on-freerunner/browse_thread/thread/e7c0c4f7600e166e

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Expected output : not such a huge drop, maybe an algorithm to check that the battery level wasn't much higher only seconds ago, and warn the user, or something :-p

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? GTA02v5 Freerunner, date code 20080618, "unpatched" (no GPS fix, no buzzfix, ...) I have noticed this since at least weekly 45+
