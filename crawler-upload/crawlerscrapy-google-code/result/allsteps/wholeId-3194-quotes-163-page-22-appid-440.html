csipsimple - issue #1616

Mangled image on incoming call on ICS if waking up

Posted on Mar 6, 2012 by Happy Monkey

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Install sandvold's ICS beta for Desire (http://www.sandvold.as/) 2.Make the phone sleep 3.Make incoming SIP call

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Answer/reject screen should appear Instead the screen lights up but the image is mangled http://imgur.com/72WnB and there is no slider. After pressing power button twice (off and then on again) the normal image appears and I can answer or reject the call.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? r1306 on sandvol'd ICS Beta The hardware is HTC Desire.

Please provide any additional information below. On GSM calls, ICS answer/reject screen appears all right (can we have the ICS theme in CSipSImple please? It's much nicer than gingerbread). Ofter installing the "AOSP theme" in CSipSimple, the answer/reject screen shows up correctly (the "old" GB-style one).

Not really a big deal since the ICS build is not really suitable for prime time use. Still I decided to file it for reference.
