Comment #3

Posted on May 13, 2011 by Quick Hippo

Sounds there is also problems with upgrade according some reports I get by mail. Probably not a good idea to move csipsimple to sdcard. I'm wondering if that's not due to something with the way android manage it more than something I do.

Having the app on sdcard means having the native library on sdcard which is obviously less performant (access time could be higher depending of filesystem on the sdcard).

Besides the Sdcard could be unmounted from the system by the android OS when it wants. In this case the app will not work at all.
