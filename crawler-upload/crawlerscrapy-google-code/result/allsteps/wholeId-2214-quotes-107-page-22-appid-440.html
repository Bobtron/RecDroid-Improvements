csipsimple - issue #2637

Incoming/Outgoing calls cause audio_msm_suspend wakelocking CPU until reboot

Posted on Feb 3, 2014 by Massive Rabbit

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Receive or make a call 2. Disconnect call 3. Wakelock persists until reboot

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

Wakelock caused by audio_msm_suspend would be released, instead it persists until reboot

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system?

Happens on both stable and latest nightly. Using Cyanogen 10 on Xperia ION.

Please provide any additional information below.

After ending incoming or outgoing call, a process audio_msm_suspend is shown in better battery stats as causing 100% wakelock draining the battery. Tapping account widget (disable/enable) does not get rid of the wakelock. Only reboot does.

Posted on Feb 3, 2014 by Quick Hippo

Ok, thanks for the proposal. Indeed, if you have time to dig into the issue it would be very helpful :)

The first thing to do is to check in logs produced by the app if the audio layer is properly released by the app. If there is a crash/problem while the pjmedia implementation for android tries to release the audio layer, the part in charge to release the wake lock will never be called and will produce the problem you mention.

If it appears not to be released, maybe logs around the audio device release might help to understand what happen. Often with problematics rom there is something with opensl-es implementation and switching to java-jni audio implementation (in csipsimple audio-troubleshooting) help to underline the problem comes from opensles support.
