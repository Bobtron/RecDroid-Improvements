csipsimple - issue #1831

Callcentric : no Audio on Incoming Calls (Outgoing works)

Posted on Jul 18, 2012 by Happy Lion

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Receive incoming call 2. Answer It

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Expected output is for audio to heard from the headset earpiece, speaker, or bluetooth ear piece.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Galaxy Nexus (GSM) Jelly Bean 4.1.1 csipsimple r1710 SIP Provider: CallCentric

Please provide any additional information below. I can place outgoing calls and everything works fine. I can hear the person I am talking to and that person can hear me. When receiving an incoming call the person I am attempting to talk to can hear me, but I can not hear them.

I have toggled STUN and ICE on without much success. However, it is my understanding that because I'm going through a proxy (callcentric) NAT is not an issue.

I have turned off bluetooth without any luck. I have rebooted the phone with bluetooth disabled, no luck. I am open to any ideas.

Please let me know what additional data you need from me.

Comment #14

Posted on Jul 21, 2012 by Quick Hippo

Mmmm, after analysis I was maybe I mistake on DNS SRV option : it seems to have an impact. Actually CSipSimple and Zoiper are not talking to the same Callcentric server. While CSipSimple talks to regular callcentric entry point on port 5060, if it does a dns srv resolution it will talk to an alpha server of callcentric clusters on port 5080. So it changes more things that what I thought. However, I'm still not sure it will help a lot.
