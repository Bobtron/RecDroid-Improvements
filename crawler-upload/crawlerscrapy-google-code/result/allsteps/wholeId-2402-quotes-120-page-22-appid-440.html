csipsimple - issue #2423

HTC Sensation XL silent mic

Posted on Jul 3, 2013 by Grumpy Horse

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Set up SIP account 2. Call somewhere 3. Try to communicate

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? I want my speaking heard by other side of call

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system? Sensation XL, ICS 4.0.3, tryed latest trunk, earliest trunk on site, stable version

Please provide any additional information below.

I am using server with SRTP and TLS enabled features, I am setting up phones for clients, made many HTC Sensation XL work with PBX by CSIPSImple with same config, but this time I am stuck - every setting set as many times before, it is always workes as intended by default(on some builds of csipsimple I had to disable QoS and RFC5626)

Now I have HTC Sensation XL that dont want to work properly with csipsimple. On default settings speaker is sillent and only if you blow to the mic hole on the bottom of phone - you will hear static. If you speak loud to mic directly - you will hear heavily damaged quiet voice.

I tryed to play with mic_source and sip mode - best result with in_call mode, but in this mode voice sounds dumb and supports with constant noise.

Had thought about broken mic - but voice recorder app records loud and clear! I will test now in gsm mode with simcard.
