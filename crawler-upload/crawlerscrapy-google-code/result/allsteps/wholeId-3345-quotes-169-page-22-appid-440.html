csipsimple - issue #1445

Continuous ring notification

Posted on Dec 19, 2011 by Helpful Bird

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Initiate calls to the phone's normal number and SIP extension simultaneously. 2. Do not answer the calls, allow terminate them from the originating phones. 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Unanswered call should be added to the call log, and notifications should stop. Instead, the log is updated properly and on-screen notification stops, but the csipsimple ring tone continues to sound until the phone is rebooted.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? CSipSimple 0.03-01 r1108 on Android 2.3.5.

Please provide any additional information below. In my particular situation, I have a Google Voice number that calls both my cell phone and my desk phone at work. The phone system is programmed to ring my SIP extension when I receive a call at work. The result is that calling my Google Voice number causes GV to ring my phone, and our Mitel system to ring my SIP extension simultaneously. When I answer from my desk phone, CSipSimple should register a missed call and stop ringing, but the CSipSimple ring tone continues until the phone is rebooted.

Posted on May 9, 2012 by Helpful Bird

Until recently, my cellular reception in the building was very poor, which is why I had it set up that way. The thing is, I had different ringtones for regular calls vs SIP calls, and it was only the SIP ringtone that would play continuously.

I have since removed the GV forward to my cell.

The way I have it set up is like this (we have a Mitel 5000 CP system): Each SIP device will need a Category F license. In the DB programming, create a new SIP device and set the passcode. In CSipSimple, create an account (I used the advanced wizard, others may work). Enter the following settings:

Server - :5060 Username - Password -

Account name and Caller ID can be whatever. Hit save and it should register.
