Hi,

can you also send logs from csipsimple app (see HowToCollectLogs wiki page). It will allow to see if csipsimple receive the IM packet and help to continue with more efficient investigations.

If the app does not receive the IM packet, it could be something with configuration of the app or with a firewall/router in the middle that refuse to transmit the IM packet If the app receive the packet, there is maybe something with how it detects the account in use (as the "From" field does not contains the port it might lost the app while trying to resolve originating account, and in this case a configuration in expert account wizard could help to workaround a potential bug here)
