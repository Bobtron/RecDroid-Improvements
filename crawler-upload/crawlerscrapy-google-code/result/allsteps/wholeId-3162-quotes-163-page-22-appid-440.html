csipsimple - issue #1689

Phone mutes when dialing when bluetooth is on (not connected)

Posted on Apr 18, 2012 by Swift Lion

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Turn on bluetooth (do not connect) 2. Dial a number (every number will do) 3. Phone goes into silent mode (vibrate)

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? I expected to make a normal phone call. Sometimes I can call, but need to set my phone to unmute. Sometimes it won't make a call, but only mutes my phone. When I turn off bluetooth I can call like expected.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? I user csipsimple version 0.03-01 on a Samsung Galaxy S2 with stock ICS (PDA: I9100XWLP7, Phone: I9100XXLPS, CSC: I9100XENLP3). Rooted my phone with CF-Root 5.4. I connect to a SIP from my office (basic).

Please provide any additional information below.
