csipsimple - issue #958

Phone hangs after latest Market update

Posted on May 12, 2011 by Quick Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Carry Droid2 around all day, in WiFi and 3G modes 2. Phone randomly hangs requiring a battery pull to reset 3.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Csipsimple 0.02-01 on System Version.2.3.20.A9555.Verizon.en.US model DROID2 Android 2.2 Baseband BP_C_01.09.07P Kernel 2.5.32.9-g462500f dbretzm1@il93lnxdroid22 #1 Build number VZW ERI version 5 PRL version 40074

Please provide any additional information below.

I have no way to be certain the problem is with Csipsimple, but after the latest Market update I started having several hangs a day and I have not (YET) had a hang since I turned off the networks in csipsimple. The hangs started after I updated about eight applications so I have been disabling or removing all of them one by one until the hangs stop.

I have not changed any settings in Csipsimple; I am not sure whether the new version works correctly as I have not received a voip call.

If you have a suggestion for a way I can be more certain whether the hangs are being caused by Csipsimple please let me know!

Is it possible the binary blob didn't get updated correctly and I need to remove and reinstall the application?

Comment #1

Posted on May 13, 2011 by Quick Hippo

Normally if the binary didn't get updated correctly the app won't start at all.

Did you move CSipSimple install on Sdcard? Some users get problems with this option while updating. (but result was not working at all, no hangs).

The best way to do is probably : * Save your accounts settings (in account view, press menu and backup). It will backup on sdcard your accounts config. * Uninstall the app. Leave csipsimple uninstalled one day and see if hangs still occurs. If not try to re-install csipsimple from the android market and restore your settings.

Let me know how it goes, if after fresh reinstall you still have issues I'd be very interested by some logs (see HowToCollectLogs wiki page).
