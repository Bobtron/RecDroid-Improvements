csipsimple - issue #229

Program won't exit if SIP account not active

Posted on Sep 20, 2010 by Happy Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. De-activate SIP profile in "Accounts" 2. Press "Back" button on phone 3. Repeat 2

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Return to dialer screen (previous versions did this). Accounts screen just refreshes self over and over.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? build 35. Froyo, CM6 on HTC Supersonic/EVO
