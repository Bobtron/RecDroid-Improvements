What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Install r1055, no matter whether through a clean install, or over a previous release. 2. Accounts stay 'inactive' (yellow bar under icon). 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Registration, as usual.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? r1055 on a Motorola Defy flashed with Gingerbread.

Please provide any additional information below. The first time I installed r1055, the issue appeared.
