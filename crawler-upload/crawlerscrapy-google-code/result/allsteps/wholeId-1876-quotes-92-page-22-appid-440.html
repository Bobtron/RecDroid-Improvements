csipsimple - issue #2756

CSipSimple network communication stops after a while.

Posted on Aug 11, 2014 by Helpful Monkey

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Connect to SIP server with CSipSimple. 2. Wait... 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? CSipSimple's registration stays up. But, it falls off the server.

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system? r2427 on a Huawei Y300-0100, running Android 4.1.1.

Please provide any additional information below. Not observed with the same build on a Huawei G300 (U8815), running Android 4.0.3.

I have registration set for 120s, keepalives every 15s. I ran tcpdump on the server, watching the registrations and keepalives, then after a while they just stop happening. CSipSimple still thinks it's registered, but there's no keepalive traffic or registrations. Disabling and re-enabling the account gets things flowing again - for a while.

Tested both over a remote WiFi connection and over a VPN. On the VPN I kept a 5-second ping running, pinging the phone. The VPN endpoint offers direct routing to the VoIP server, bi-directional communication is possible without NAT.
