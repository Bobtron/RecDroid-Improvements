csipsimple - issue #1871

HTC One XL on ICS cannot place calls

Posted on Aug 12, 2012 by Happy Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Install any version after about 1590 2.Configure a Pennytel VOIP provider 3.Make a SIP call

What is the expected output? Normal call to connect What do you see instead? Call in Progress . . . 500 / Disconnected The disconnect happens immediately after the call attempt.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Android 4.0.3 on Telstra (Australia). CSipSimple versions 1590, 1600, latest (12 Aug 2012), 1767.

Please provide any additional information below. Tried with bluetooth enabled / disabled, same results. No accounts configured in native SIP config. VOIP shows as registered. I originally had the issue resolved with r1633 but it re-surfaced after an update - tried on 3G as well as WiFi and cannot get it working again.
