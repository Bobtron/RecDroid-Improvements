Wallpaper Wizard: Cartesian Genetic Programming for Customized Android Wallpaper

Project Description

"I don't know anything about art, but I know what I like." - Artist Gelett Burgess

The Wallpaper Wizard is a program that generates abstract pictures which are perfect for the background of smartphones. By using the artificial intelligence concept of cartesian genetic programming, this Android application learns the user's preferences in design and color to generate and remix a collection of pieces tailored to their likes and dislikes.

Written by Kathryn Hardey, 2010 for CSC440 at Centenary College of Louisiana.

All proceeds generated from the ads will benefit the computer science students in travel and registration for conferences and competitions.
