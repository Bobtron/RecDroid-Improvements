hovi

Android Bluetooth RC Hovercraft Project

This Project was started as course project from Computer Engineering Project(PEC), that's taught in Facultat d'Informàtica de Barcelona - Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (FIB - UPC)

The main goal is to familiarize with planning and development of an embedded systems and design of low-cost low-power autonomous systems.

In this semester we decide to design and develop an Hovercraft. We have made every piece of plastic with a 3D printer except that the base is made with wood. The hovercraft is controlled from an Android device. It can take pictures and send it to device. And The LEDs are used for Knight Rider's effect

The main components are:

  • PIC18
  • 2 Brushless DC Motors
  • 2 Propeller
  • 1 Servo
  • Parts made ​​from 3D printer
  • Bluetooth module
  • 2 IR Sensor
  • 1 Camera module
  • Android App
  • 15 LEDs
