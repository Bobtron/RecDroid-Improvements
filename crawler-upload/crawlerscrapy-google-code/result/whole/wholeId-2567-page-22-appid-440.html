Comment #1

Posted on May 6, 2013 by Quick Hippo

It was due to a bug in android 4.x and selection of preferred activities with custom intents content. A workaround has been introduced in the app. An app that which to become the preferred call activity should use the preference : SipConfigManager.CALL_UI_PACKAGE now (would be great if such apps ask users if they want to use their call ui).
