android-on-freerunner - issue #25

Removing battery from FreeRunner results in Android no longer suspending

Posted on Sep 29, 2009 by Happy Dog

NB: This is with Michael's image, but I suspect the same will be true for the new ones as well.

I sometimes get a situation where my phone will ring but not activate the display to let me reject the call and sometimes in desperation I pull the battery to stop it ringing (say I've forgotten to put it into silent mode - I'll report that separately). After that the phone will no longer suspend.

I know the answer is "don't do that" but it'd be great to be able to have a fix in case people run into situations where it locks up and the battery has to be removed.

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Pull battery while phone is ringing 2. Turn phone on again 3. After a while of thinking it has been suspended check with SpareParts Battery Info and see that it's been running for 100% of the time.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

Phone should not show running as 100%. But it says 100%

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system?

This is with Michael Trimarchi's v15 image.
