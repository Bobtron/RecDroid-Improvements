Comment #2

Posted on Jul 27, 2011 by Quick Hippo

My bad, after re-reading the code, actually 4G is taken into account with the 3G option. In fact it is "3G or more" (CDMA, LTE etc are included in 3G option).

If you want to have a list of supported types : http://developer.android.com/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyManager.html#NETWORK_TYPE_1xRTT

All greater than 3 are considered as 3G. So issue 1179 is not linked to this one, and this one is just about naming of the "3G option".
