csipsimple - issue #883

Cannot come back to call after back key or home key press

Posted on Apr 12, 2011 by Happy Rhino

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Call someone 2. Press back key or home key while calling 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? A button is needed to come back to the call if we press the return key or the home key. We can come back to the call by the notification slide (which say that a call is running) but i think a button in the app (or a movement to the left or the right) should be a good thing. The first time i experienced this, i finally reboot my phone to stop the call...

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Nighty build 2011-04-12 r790

Please provide any additional information below.

Comment #1

Posted on Apr 12, 2011 by Quick Hippo

That's not the user experience on android.

It works exactly the same way using the stock phone application on android.

If we were on iPhone, I would say yes, but here that's not needed. The notification bar is enough to access the current call again and that's the way it is usually done on android. That's the purpose of these kind of ongoing notifications.

Besides, one user (and also forks of csipsimple or apps that use csipsimple as service only) may not use the csipsimple "launcher" at all. It's just a dialer, not the sip telephony module.

I think your request comes more to the fact you does not have yet android reflex than to something really missing in the UI. Besides sliding is a power user action that will be found by 1% of guys using the app.

Comment #2

Posted on Apr 13, 2011 by Happy Rhino

Hi,

The android user experience is different if you are using the "real" android or an interface like HTC sense. I am using HTC sense since about half and a year. Let me explain my point of view : With the default phone (not sip), if you go to home while a call, the easiest and more ergonomic way to come back to the call is to press the "Phone" button in the home page. I think that if there is a call running with CSispSimple, a launch of the app (with a shorcut in the home page for example) or a press on the phone button in the app (the green phone) should comme back to the call. Going to the notification slide is not obvious. There should be a way in the app to do it. I am suggesting one, maybe not the best one but it is a way to do it.
