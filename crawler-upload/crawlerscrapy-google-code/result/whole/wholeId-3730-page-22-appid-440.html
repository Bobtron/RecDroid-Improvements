About the GSM problem : I guess the problem is on server side. Both ends (server or remote party depending on what does the server) must support the codec to have it working. In my experience with betamax clones, the delay you note depends on the quality of the connection. Usually delay in my case is not present also in 3G. On my HTC Legend, in expert settings I set GSM or g729 codec, disabling echo cancellation, voice quality 3 and clock rate 8Khz. only udp transport enabled for Betamax clones and Stun enabled.

I hope will be useful
