csipsimple - issue #2216

CSipSimple disables all sound

Posted on Feb 2, 2013 by Massive Giraffe

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Make a SIP call. 2. Finish the call. 3. Do anything which generates sound (e.g. press a key, play a sound file, call from another phone, make outgoing call...).

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Expected: Everything works normally (key sound, incoming call sound, call voice, media sound...). Current result: In many cases (more than 50 %) is all sound disabled. Nothing can be heard at all. Additionally, all volume controls are set on zero and cannot be adjusted (they return back to zero).

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? CSipSimple - latest from Google Play (2013-01-15) Android 2.3.6, device Samsung Galaxy W (GT-I8150)

Please provide any additional information below. The described problem (no sound) can be resolved only be switching the phone off and then back on.
