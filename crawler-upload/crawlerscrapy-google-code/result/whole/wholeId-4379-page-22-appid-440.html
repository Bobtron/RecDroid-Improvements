csipsimple - issue #406

Cannot dial with international prefix "+"

Posted on Nov 22, 2010 by Happy Giraffe

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Dial an international prefixed with "+", e.g. +4930999999999 2. Pick up 3. csipsimple immediately hangs up / without any communication with asterisk

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Be able to call numbers with international prefix. Interestingly if you use the double zero notation ( e.g. 004930999999999) it works.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Android 2.1, csipsimple 0.00-15

Please provide any additional information below.
