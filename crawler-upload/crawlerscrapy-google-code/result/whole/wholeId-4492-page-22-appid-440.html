Is that possible for you to send me logs just before these ones (you can send it by mail to avoid to publish your private infos on the website). Cause, here there is not what happen just before we receive a BYE request from the remote party.

But as far as I can tell regarding your logs provided here, your remote party has send to you (csipsimple) a BYE request, which mean they want to terminate the call... So absolutely normal that csipsimple terminate the call in this case.

In some case, the remote party could ask that cause there is a problem on their side (for example no codec found to manage this communication). So maybe some clue in logs that are just before these ones.
