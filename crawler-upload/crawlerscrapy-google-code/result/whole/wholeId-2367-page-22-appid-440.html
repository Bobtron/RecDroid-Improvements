csipsimple - issue #2450

Does not register on mobile network since 23 July 2013. It registers via wi-fi though.

Posted on Jul 26, 2013 by Helpful Hippo

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Start CSipSimple 2.Ensure good mobile reception 3.Attempt to register a SIP account over the mobile network

What is the expected output?

The SIP account registers (as it did up to and including 23 July 2013)

What do you see instead?

The app times out (trying to connect to to the SIP account), and is unable to register.

What version of the product are you using?

1.01.00 r2272

On what device / operating system?

Samsung GT-I9100T Android 4.1.2

Please provide any additional information below.

It currently works, and has always worked, fine on WiFi. It worked fine on the mobile network up to and including 23 July 2013. It hasn't worked on the mobile network since then.

Comment #4

Posted on Jul 26, 2013 by Quick Hippo

Can you elaborate more the symptoms you have. What do you mean by "it doesn't register"? Is there some 408 timeout error after some time it tries? Does it fails on registration or on calls?

If so the problem is a 408 on registration/call, it is probably due to the fact UDP packets sent by the app might be a little bit big (as the app as many features to announces to the server) and some mobile network have very limited udp packets limits. Trying to enable "use compact sip" option might be helpful.

If the problem is some different error code, the logs (see HowToCollectLogs wiki page) could be helpful to understand the root cause (maybe a change from you SIP provider on what they expect as sip configuration -- for example one feature they do not support correctly anymore)
