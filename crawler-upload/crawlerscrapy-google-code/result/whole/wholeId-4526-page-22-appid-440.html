csipsimple - issue #270

No voice issue with a certain SIP provider(s).

Posted on Oct 9, 2010 by Grumpy Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Connect to the Internet through NAT-enabled environment. 2. Make a call.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The both parties should be able to talk without any problem. But you'd hear nothing from the other party and the other party hears you clearly.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.00-13 (Android Market version) / Froyo 2.2 on HTC Desire.

Please provide any additional information below. Seems like it happens only with my primary SIP provider and only with CSipSimple. (Never had such problem with another Android clients such as Sipdroid, 3CX, etc)

I've sent related logs to your E-mail address. Please check. Thanks in advance.

And another question : are you sure there is no other sip client running (someone recently had problems with voice because sipdroid was running in background and blocking some udp ports !)

Comment #8

Posted on Oct 22, 2010 by Quick Hippo

Just for reference, I reverted the automatic STUN since it prevent some users to register on some countries and with some providers. As it's more annoying than no sound in one way under NATed network, I disabled STUN (but keep the default stun server... so users will just have to tick enable STUN to solve the problem). Besides as the problem is pretty easy to diagnose since audio is just one way, it's matter of documentation now. I'll probably add a webview that link the FAQ of the website to explain users how to solve the one-way-audio issue.
