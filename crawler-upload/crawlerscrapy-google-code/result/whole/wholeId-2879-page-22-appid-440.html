Comment #1

Posted on Aug 24, 2012 by Quick Hippo

You can choose between clever or not method by : 1 - Switch to expert setting mode (see : http://code.google.com/p/csipsimple/wiki/ExpertSettingMode?wl=en#General_settings 2 - Go in user interface section. The two first checkboxes are about integration.

If you have no problem with the clever integration keep it enabled. The other integration is only needed when other apps such as skype or viber are also installed on the device and that you don't "remember choice" when dialing. So in your case, if was fine previously just Check first option (clever integration). Uncheck second option (priviledged integration). And ignore the warning you have in the warning tab. It's just a warning and text is important. When I'm sure a setting is better for you/your device it's automatically turned on or off. There is cases where I'm not sure and that requires human reflection. In this case I ask :).
