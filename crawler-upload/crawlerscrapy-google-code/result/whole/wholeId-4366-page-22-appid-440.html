Comment #12

Posted on Dec 5, 2010 by Quick Hippo

TCP & UDP were both enabled in the Expert menu for Network, while the Transport in the account properties was set to "Auto". I disabled TCP and tried to log in again - no luck. New error in logcat:

You should not disable TCP in Network settings (TCP can still be useful for other stuff or other accounts if you have). You have to change the transport to UDP in the account. Auto in your case is not valid. If you keep Auto and disable TCP, the sip stack will have a packet to send through TCP (cause to big) and have no transport to send it. So in your case : keep TCP and UDP transport enabled. and in account setting instead of Transport "Auto", choose transport "UDP".

Also try to use 00.15-18 that may help for Hung up. (But I think that in this case it's also linked to the transport "auto" setting in account settings).
