textadventure - issue #18

Have a way to say whether a door on a corner will appear on the horizontal or vertical wall

Posted on Jan 26, 2011 by Helpful Elephant

When a door is drawn in a corner exit, it is drawn on the wall residing on the left of the corner. It means that the northeast door is always drawn on the north wall, while the northwest door is drawn on the west door. This is ugly and counterintuitive. Either:

  • Make two independant exits on each corner (ie distinguish the NorthEast corner from the EastNorth corner) Note that the user may then have a problem to designate the exit he means to go, especially with gestures.

  • Indicate in the exit (or the door itself) on which one of the two walls it should be drawn
