csipsimple - issue #182

Ongoing ring tone after call terminated

Posted on Aug 30, 2010 by Happy Horse

HTC Legend - 2.1 Since -24 (and continues with -25) if a call comes to my PBX, which rings my remote extension on the Legend as part of the ring group, if the calling party hangs up before the call is answered, my Legend continues to ring until I power off the phone. I am unable to hang up from the client.

This has happened numerous times. Rolled back to -23. Great work on this SIP client, by the way!
