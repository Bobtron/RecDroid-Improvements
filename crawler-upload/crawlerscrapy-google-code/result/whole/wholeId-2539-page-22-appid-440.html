csipsimple - issue #2281

Bluetooth: GSM can not use bluetooth after CSipSimple used bluetooth connection once

Posted on Mar 11, 2013 by Swift Wombat

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Open CSipSimple 2. Make call 3. After call is established, switch on the Bluetooth connection to headset

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Bluetooth works fine in CSipSimple but afterwards (after ending the call) Bluetooth is not longer working in GSM calls (worked well before). I have to uninstall / reinstall CSipSimple or reboot the phone to make Bluetooth work with GSM calls again.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Alcatell OT 918D with Gingerbread 2.3.5 (rooted). Using nightly builds as well as 0.004-04-r1916.

Please provide any additional information below. Problem might correspond to issue 1430 but for there are GSM calls not mentioned I opend a new issue. Additional information: After CSipSimple used Bluetooth for the first time, GSM calls opens connection to headset (hearing something in the headset - but falls back to speaker after about 1 second. GSM nonetheless assumes phone is connected to BT). This only happens as long as CSipSimple is registered.
