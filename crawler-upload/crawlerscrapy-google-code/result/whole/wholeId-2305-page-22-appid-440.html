Comment #1

Posted on Oct 22, 2013 by Quick Hippo

It's dev question, so please join the csipsimple-dev google group and ask there.

Also, I deeply recommend you to not do such a version of csipsimple with g729 enabled by default, unless you have rights to distribute unlimited versions of the g729 codec patent rights to all users. I remind you that csipsimple core app is distributed under GPL license, and as consequence if you redistribute your app it should be under GPL too. And I remind you that g729 is a badly patented codec.

So, if you care about communication openness and freedom you should not do that. There is much more open alternatives that are better than g729 (such as SILK codec).
