The contacts list search is available if you press the txt button (it should be on bottom right of the dialer). You'll not have direct full list (I've removed it for performance reason and may reintroduce it in the future once performance problem is solved ;) ). But you can search and it will automatically list contacts that match the search (it search with name, phone number or internet call field of contacts).
