csipsimple - issue #589

csipsimple does not automatically disconnect in "only for outgoing mode"

Posted on Jan 14, 2011 by Happy Bear
  1. setup csipsimple as to be used "only for outgoing" in easy config
  2. try to dial a number. csipsimple wakes up automatically - an icon can be seen on the upper statusbar. do your conversation, whatever, no matter if on mobile or through sip
  3. end conversation and wait... and wait...

Csipsimple icon should disappear, because it should wake up only for outgoing calls. Instead, it remains there and eats up your battery. You can still be happy that you are sip-available all the time, even though you have set it up as "only for outgoing". You can "turn off" your csipsimple by going into the application and using the "disconnect" button. Sometimes it works just to go into Conclusion: in daily use, you have to enter the csipsimple application and tap "disconnect" after each and every call (mobile or sip), in order not to drain your battery.

I use: csipsimple 0.00-16 r410 Android 2.2.1 w/ HTC Sense HTC Desire Z

Cheers, Horia
