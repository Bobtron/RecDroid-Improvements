csipsimple - issue #1396

Account-level connectivity preferences

Posted on Nov 23, 2011 by Happy Bird

I would like to thank the team for such a good product which csipsimple is; the only thing that's missing (in my opinion) is the ability to tie connectivity preferences and policies to every single voip account:

Let me make an example:

I have an internal asterisk @home, so when I'm on my home Wi-fi I wanto to route my calls through it, then when I'm on cellular network I want my calls routed through another account. Each account holding its own nat, keep-alive policies and so on.

In the old nokia days, the internal client worked this way, which I find very convenient, this way we could have multiple accounts enabled in csipsimple, with every one kicking in when appropriate, without the need to enable,disable them in the app or via widget.

This is of course only a feature request and not an actual bug. Thank you for your patience and your good work.

Alex
