klaxon - issue #27

Response with Original Text

Posted on Aug 3, 2010 by Grumpy Monkey

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Get a page from a TelAlert system that has a ticket number in it 2. Reply to the text and TelAlert will not acknowledge it

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Would like to be able to reply with the original Subject line from the page in the first line of the acknowledge page

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? V 0.21 Android 2.2 on a Motorola Droid with VZW

The only reason why I ask this is a lot of people in our IT dept are switching from BB and iPhone over to the Androids and everyone here is using Klaxon for our paging software. Currently if we get a page we have to switch over to our SMS app and then reply back and put the ticket number that comes in the subject line into the body of the sms and then our acknowledge string. It would be nice if there was an option to tell Klaxon to either look for a substring in the original page and then stick that in the canned reply or just an option to include subj in reply. Thanks.

Please provide any additional information below.
