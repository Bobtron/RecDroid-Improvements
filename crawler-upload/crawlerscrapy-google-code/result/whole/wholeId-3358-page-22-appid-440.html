csipsimple - issue #1458

Request: Option to answer with speakerphone as part of filters

Posted on Dec 29, 2011 by Happy Cat

I'd like to be able to answer with speakerphone if a certain condition is met in the filter.

The reason for this is I want my phone using Csipsimple to be able to be paged or intercommed from another phone on my VoIP system.

If my desk phone is 101 and my cell phone is 102, I could set a filter where if the caller is 101, then the phone automatically answers with speakerphone. That way, someone can "page" me easily, without me having to pick up the phone.

Comment #1

Posted on May 29, 2012 by Quick Hippo

This can now be done with combination of auto answer filter and one of the following new option : * Auto rear speaker. If so the phone will automatically use rear spearker Or better in your case I think : * Detect rear speaker use. This last one is just based on the phone orientation. So if your phone is horizontal, it will be on rear speaker, and if vertical near your ear it will automatically switch to earpiece.

Alternatively this feature could be performed by a third party application that may manage the in call activity a different way. But in your case I think that the auto-detect rear speaker need is a good solution.

Comment #2

Posted on Aug 9, 2015 by Quick Horse

IMHO the auto answer feature should automatically invoke the rear speaker.

The two solutions that were proposed are not ideal for the following reasons:

Auto rear speaker means that I get rear speaker automatically, even when I manually answer a call. When I manually answer a call, I never want the rear speaker. However, if I enable auto answer, it is because I want the phone answered without any user intervention. The phone will not be up near my head at that time.

Detect rear speaker use is also not idea because I may want auto answer to work even when my phone is in my pocket. If the phone is in my pocket and auto-answers, I need the volume to be loud so that I can hear that a person has called me.
