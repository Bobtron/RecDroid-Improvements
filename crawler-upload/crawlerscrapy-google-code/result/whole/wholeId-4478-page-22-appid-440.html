What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Start app 2.sign in to asterisk account 3.issue a call to any asterisk extension

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Dell streak hangs. Sometimes removal of battery is needed and sometimes you can manage the application and stop it. In this case csipsimple nor any other sip client is able to register until a hard reset is issued.

Comment #1

Posted on Oct 23, 2010 by Quick Hippo

Ok, sounds to be an ANR (Application Not Responding), not a crash.

Multiple things could cause that. But I don't think that's link to your device (some said me that CSipSimple is working properly with Dell Steak).

So first though that come : * Could be something with the codec. Some codecs eat a lot of CPU, you could try to disable all codecs except PCMU and PCMA on settings > Media > Codecs (long click to disable a codec) * Could be a dead lock in the underlying SIP stack due to a specific flow from the asterisk server. But I don't think so, pjsip (the sip stack) is reliable. * Could be something with the driver version of the Dell Steak. What android version is it? It's maybe a different one than the one it has been reported to me it works.
