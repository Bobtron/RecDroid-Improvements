csipsimple - issue #959

CSIPSimple hungs with a Terrasip account if STUN is not enabled

Posted on May 13, 2011 by Swift Rhino

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Disable STUN 2. Configure a Terrasip (terrasip.com) account [1] 3. Try to activate the Terrasip account.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

Expected: account registration.

I See: In the accounts menu you see the Terrasip account saying "Registering...". After a few seconds the phone vibrates a couple of times and Android says CSIPSimple is hung and offer to kill the app. As soon as CSIPSimple is restarted, it tries to connect and hungs again.

To recover, I switch off wifi and GPRS, start CSIPSimple, disable Terrasip account and connect wifi again.

If stun server is enabled (I use stunserver.org) Terrasip account registers OK.
