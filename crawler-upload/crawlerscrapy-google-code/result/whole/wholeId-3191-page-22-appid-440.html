csipsimple - issue #1613

CSIP Dialer option raised _after_ call Samsung / Motorola

Posted on Mar 4, 2012 by Grumpy Cat

If you choose to integrate the csip dialer with android and make a call with the stock dialer you are supposed to get the options dialog asking you to make the call with either stock gsm or csip sip. This has worked in the past. With the latest nightlies this has changed. The options dialog is not raised, the call is being placed with the stock dialer. However once you hang up, the stock dialer puts you back into stock call history and shows you the gsm/csip options dialog there - though after hangup... It is supposed to do that before the call ;)

Devices: Samsung Galaxy Note 2.3.6 CheckromHD / Motorola Pro+ 2.3.6 stock Rom. Both with stock dialers.
