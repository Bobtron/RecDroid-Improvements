If you have a data plan, GPRS should work on most carriers, although I'm seeing issues where GPRS doesn't start up after losing my wifi signal. If you're having problems, please make sure you have an APN defined with proper information for your carrier in Settings/Wireless Controls/Mobile Networks/Access Point Names. Could you report: - Whether you see an APN for your carrier in the Settings app. - Whether the settings match what your carrier recommends.

If it's just a matter of your carrier's settings being missing in the installed image, attach a comment here listing the settings that work, and we will add them to the build.
