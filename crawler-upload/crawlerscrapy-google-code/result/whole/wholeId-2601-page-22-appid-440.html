csipsimple - issue #2225

No sound in normal call (GSM call) after making a csipsimple call

Posted on Feb 6, 2013 by Helpful Dog

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Make a GSM call and can hear voice in both sides. 2.Hang up the GSM call and make a voip call by Csipsimple, in this case both sides can hear each other. 3.Hang up the voip call and make a GSM call again, at this time no voice in both sides.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? In step 3, should hear voice in both sides, but no voice can be heard in both sides.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.04-04 r1916

Please provide any additional information below. Mobile phone: Coolpad 9120 Android version: 4.0.4 Log in this case: 02-06 10:13:14.869: E/AudioHardware(129): AudioStreamInVoip::read(0x42288544, 320) 02-06 10:13:14.869: E/AudioHardware(129): AudioStreamInVoip::read : device is already open 02-06 10:13:14.869: E/AudioHardware(129): Calling read count = 320 mBufferSize = 320 02-06 10:13:14.889: E/AudioHardware(129): read_bytes = 328 mvs
