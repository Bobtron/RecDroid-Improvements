Mmmm strange on my SGS I've not such a noise. (The problem I have with SGS is the recorded sound but that's cause of crappy samsung audio driver for recording, but no real problem with listening).

What is the remote party? Did you try with other sip clients with the same remote party? Also is there other running apps on the system that could lock audio (a non standard music player, another sip app...)? You could try to reboot the phone if something else hold the audio resources.

Can you also test an echo test on your sip provider. It could be a good test to see whether problems comes from your side or the other side, or some problematic DSP on your provider side.
