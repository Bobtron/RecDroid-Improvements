Comment #1

Posted on Jun 19, 2011 by Quick Hippo

Actually what you have on normal call is not a feature of android but one of the manufacturer (I guess HTC). I even think this has a patent.

I plan to do something different in csipsimple as auto speaker feature. Read my comments on issue 662. And participate on the debate on the issue 662.

Keep in mind that a feature in CSipSimple should be available for as most device as possible, so I think that the approach you describe will not apply all devices and besides may require the app read g-sensor which are not equally implemented on all devices. It can become quickly a source of headache to implement this kind of feature cross devices.

But the idea is interesting, but for now I keep thinking a good approach would be the one I describe in issue 662. (Option to have auto speaker based on proximity sensor). Besides AFAIK this one is not patented... (well, in U.S., sounds every idea is patented lol...)
