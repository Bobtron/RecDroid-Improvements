csipsimple - issue #1499

Audio not working on one side since new codes

Posted on Jan 15, 2012 by Grumpy Cat

Expected: Before the new codecs/video etc around r116x were introduced audio quality was good.

Bug: Since those releases only one side of the call is almost impossible to understand. Switching audio codec away from the standard g722 to speex solves this.

Tested on multiple Samsung 2.3.6 + CM 2.3.7. Everything is fine prior to r1156.
