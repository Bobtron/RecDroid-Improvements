csipsimple - issue #1674

Excessive battery consumption when connected over 3G network

Posted on Apr 11, 2012 by Grumpy Kangaroo

This is observed using an Nexus One (AWS network) running either CM7 (2.3.7) or ICS (4.0.4). Both Market versions and Nightly versions produce same behavior.

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Connect to SIP server via TCP with a keep alive of 600 ms 2. Turn off display and place phone in pocket (leave idle) in an area with excellent signal reception.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? I expect to see very low battery usage as the keep alive is 600 ms and the phone is not being used. What I see is at least a 10-15% battery drop per hour in this state. As soon as I close CSipSimple, battery usage goes back to normal (1-2% per hour).

Please provide any additional information below.

I remember a time when this worked properly, and the phone could last me all day long. Not sure what has changed recently to create such a huge drain. I see that everytime any data is sent to or from the phone, my 3G icon in the status bar changes from 3G to H. When the radio is doin absolutely nothing, it says 3G. When there is any data whatsoever being sent, it switches to H. It has always been this way. At every change of state (from either 3G -> H or H -> 3G) I see log entries from csipsimple declaring that network change is detected and to do actions because of it. Is this normal behavior? Even though I'm not changing networks? Could this be a source of battery drain?
