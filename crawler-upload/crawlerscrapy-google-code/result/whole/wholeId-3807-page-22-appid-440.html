When moving from 3g to wifi - doesn't always connect

Posted on Jun 2, 2011 by Helpful Bird

Sometimes, perhaps when my phone is sleeping, I turn my phone on and see that it is not connected sip. This only seems to happen in wifi, but I'm on wifi most of the time.

In any case, opening up csip seems to re-establish connection within a couple of seconds. I once accessed the accounts screen (moments before connecting) and saw my sip account orange and marked as "inactive"?
