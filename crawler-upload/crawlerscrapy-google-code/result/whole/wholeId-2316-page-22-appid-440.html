csipsimple - issue #2459

No Audio between SIP Clients

Posted on Aug 5, 2013 by Helpful Rhino

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Register two Android phones as a SIP clients on AsteriskWin32 PBX. 2. Dial one extension from the other. 3. Talk.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The media readings register no variations for the speaker. The micro does register, but no audio is heard on either client.

The call connection is maintained, but there is no audio.

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system? Handset 1: Micromax A90, Andriod 4.0.3 Handset 2: Samsung Galaxy GT-S5360, Andriod 2.3.6 AsteriskWin32 PBX running on Windows XP, SP3, .NET4.0 CSipSimple: 1.01.00r2272

Please provide any additional information below. I was able to use the above configuration successfully with Ozeki Phone System.

I am able to hear audio through the PBX echo test, but no audio is recorded. The mic and speaker media graphs both register variations during this, but no audio is recorded.

I have tried the following fixes: a. WebRTC. b. Samsung Galaxy Hack. c. Tone Hack. d. Setup audio before init. e. Audio mode for SIP: Normal f. Mic sources: Default, Mic and Voice_Communication g. Activating/deactivating echo cancellation, noise reduction. h. Changing the audio channel frequency. i. Changing the codecs. j. Changing the mic, speaker volumes. k. Enabling QoS on packets. l. Enabling compact SIP.

But, no luck with any of the above.
