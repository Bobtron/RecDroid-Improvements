csipsimple - issue #2331

Voice Mail for Voip.ms should be *97

Posted on Apr 20, 2013 by Quick Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Left myself a voice mail on my voip.ms number 2. CsipSimple showed a voice mail (set up using the voip.ms setup wizard) 3. when I chose the voice mail alert on my phone, CsipSimple said it couldn't make the call 4. I switched to the Expert wizard and added *97 as the voice mail number 5. After doing that CsipSimple was able to make the voice mail call

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

https://code.google.com/p/csipsimple/wiki/FAQ#I_did_a_mistake_when_I_set_up_the_voice_mail_number,_how_can_I_m

I used the "Expert" wizard to add *97 as the voice mail number; the FAQ suggests it should be built in to the Voip.ms wizard.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system?

Latest Android version from Google Play on 4/20/2013.
