csipsimple - issue #295

Option to have double tap screen instead of slide locker screen

Posted on Oct 20, 2010 by Swift Rhino

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. turn on Keep awake while on call 2. make call 3. cover proximity sensor

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Screen should dim. Screen dims and semi-transparent slide unlocker appears. slide unlocker appears regardless the setting of "Don't use slide unlocker".

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? CSipSimple 0.00-15 HTC EVO 2.2 build 3.29.651.5 CL252548 release-keys

Please provide any additional information below.

Comment #1

Posted on Oct 20, 2010 by Quick Hippo

Normal behavior :

"Don't use slide unlocker" > That's for taking call (it allow you to choose between slide to take call and 2 simple buttons).

HTC EVO has a problem with WIFI + screen off (that's a known android bug) => What you observe (dimmed screen + overlay locker) is a workaround that allow you to have a good call quality (dimmed screen) and avoid you to make unwanted action with your face when near to the screen. On devices without this bug proximity sensor just behaves as it should (it turn screen off).

Just try to manually turn screen off -> you'll see that sound become really choppy (wifi goes in PSP mode and doesn't receive packets as frequently as it should -> this bug is present on nexus one, desire and evo).

So what you observe is the best way to handle the limitation introduced by the HTC bug (http://code.google.com/p/android/issues/detail?id=9781).
