Posted on Jan 27, 2013 by Quick Hippo

Hi,

Most of the time the "488/not acceptable here" error occurs when codec list is not compatible between sip server and sip client.

Logs (see HowToCollectLogs wiki page) from an incoming call could help to determine which are the codecs enabled on server and should be enabled in csipsimple (settings > media > codecs). Or if your sip provider gives instructions to configure sip client they probably give the list of codecs they support.
