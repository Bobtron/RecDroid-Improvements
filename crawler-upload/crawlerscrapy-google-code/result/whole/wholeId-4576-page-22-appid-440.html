csipsimple - issue #239

Bluetooth volume

Posted on Sep 23, 2010 by Helpful Bird

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Make call with bluetooth 2. Try desperately to hear really quite audio 3. Note that attempting to turn up volume only turns up media volume, which is already on full.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? See Bluetooth volume, not media volume.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Nexus 2.2 CSIP 36

Please provide any additional information below. Sometimes, but only very briefly whilst bluetooth is being enabled, I see the Bluetooth volume appear with has the volume set to almost nothing, but it disappears before it adjusts back up.

Thanks.
