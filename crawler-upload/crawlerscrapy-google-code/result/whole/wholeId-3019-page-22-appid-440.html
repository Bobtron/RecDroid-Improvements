csipsimple - issue #1785

Suggestions/enhacements

Posted on Jun 14, 2012 by Massive Ox

First, I'd like say that this is a wonderful SIP app for Android... but of course there's always room for improvement. Here are a couple of suggestions:

  1. Using my new Samsung Galazy Blaze (similar to the S2 just smaller), when I activate bluetooth there's no audio either way and after disabling bluetooth still no audio on the handset or speakerphone/ringer. Also it freezes the app and need to reboot.

  2. A simple voice mail feature would be great to pick up calls after a certain number of rings.

  3. CallerID should have 2 selectable methods to be compatible with more sip providers: Your method just sends the callerID number in the display field of the URI like this "CallerID" <sip:username@sipprovider.com> The second method should send the callerID first in the username field upon the initial invite like this:

"<blank>" <sip:CallerID@sipprovider.com> then on the authentication invite should send this: "<blank>" <sip:username@sipprovider.com>

Basically on the initial invite we send the callerID in the username field, then send the username in the username field for authentication to complete the call.

I've been using Vitelity.net for years which is great commercial grade provider for just pennies per minute and it uses the second callerID method.

Thanks, Bill

The developer has been notified and working on the above issues...

The audio outage is due to the inconsistent bluetooth stack implementation among the various android devices, you may have to try different audio/call methods under the expert settings and find the one that works for you. So far I haven't found a working one for the Samsung Blaze which is closely related to the Galaxy S2. So any Galaxy S2 users out there please share some suggestions/solutions.
