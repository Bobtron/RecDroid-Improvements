csipsimple - issue #814

By r723 CSipSimple does not remain in background after some time (40-50 min) of phone inactivity

Posted on Mar 18, 2011 by Happy Bear

In previous versions it was a problem to get rid of the csipsimple, in the "only for outgoing" mode (see issue 589). Now I am happy that it goes away very easily (for example after dialling out).

But we are in the other extreme: csipsimple "goes away" even if it shouldn't, after not using the phone for about an hour, considering that: 1. selected mode is "always available" 2. "use partial wake lock" in on (!!) Csipsimple wakes up in a rush by activating the screen, but until then you are not sip-connected anymore. This does not happen each and every time, sometimes csipsimple stays awake even after some hours of not using the phone. I notice much improved battery consumption (also because of improvements like to simultaneous reconnection of more accounts etc.) but I'm afraid it has much more to do with csipsimple taking a nap when nobody sees it :) This was not happening in previous versions where we were having a hard time getting rid of csipsimple...

Thanks! Horia

Comment #8

Posted on May 27, 2011 by Happy Bear

Hi again! Problem solved. I was using csipsimple on 3G connection on htc Desire Z. You need to activate the following, to allow internet traffic at anytime: Settings > Wireless & networks > Mobile networks > "Enable always on mobile data" ON Then it works. Otherwise the phone software cuts out the internet traffic after some time (deep sleep?), so no sip availability anymore after some time. Thanks. Consider this issue solved. Horia
