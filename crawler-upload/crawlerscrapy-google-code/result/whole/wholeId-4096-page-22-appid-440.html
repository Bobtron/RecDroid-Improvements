csipsimple - issue #694

Trying to adjust the volume before the callee accepts the call make the phone vibrate until you hang up

Posted on Feb 8, 2011 by Grumpy Ox

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Call someone 2. Increase/Decrease volume before s/he picks up 3. Your phone will vibrate as long as you don't hang up

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Endless vibration during call obviously should not happen.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? CSipSimple 0.01-01 r615 from market. Bug reproduced on Nexus One running Android 2.2.2 and HTC Tattoo with Android 1.6
