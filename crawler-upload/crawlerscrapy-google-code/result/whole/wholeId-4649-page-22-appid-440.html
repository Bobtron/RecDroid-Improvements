Ok (was in my mind since I own an archos 5IT too ;) ) Not sure there is an api to know whether phone is GSM capable but, anyway I'll find a solution at least testing the brand/device model.

Just a remark (not important at all but ;) ): 3G, GPRS, EDGE are data layers and even without data your phone can make calls. That's the reason why on your phone you have probably two indicators : one for the signal strength that correspond to the gsm signal on which voice calls relies and another that show you data channel (edge, G(prs) 3g, hdspa ... ). On this channel all ~datas~ packets are exchanged. Was just for your information ;)
