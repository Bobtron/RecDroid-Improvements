csipsimple - issue #2354

SIP Account switch widget unavailable when app moved to USB Storage

Posted on May 13, 2013 by Massive Dog

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. On android 4.0+ Go to the App Drawer 2. Drag the "SIP Account switch widget" to App Info in the top of the screen. 3. Click "Move to USB storage" button in App Info

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? I expect to still see the SIP Account switch widgets in the App Drawer. But it's gone. Widgets placed previously in the launcher show an error.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? App version 0.04-04 installed via F-Droid. Model number Nexus S Android version 4.1.2 Baseband version I9023XXKI1 Kernel 3.0.31-g5894150 android-build@vpbs1 Build number JZO54K

Please provide any additional information below. Rebooting doesn't fix it. Moving app back to phone, does.

Comment #1

Posted on May 13, 2013 by Quick Hippo

It's a bug in android launcher/ROM. Moving the app to SDcard is highly discouraged unless you are sure it doesn't introduce problems on your ROM. On many devices/rom, it introduces with network permissions for example; could also with widget (some ROM considers an app on the sdcard should not add something on the home screen because might be disabled in the sdcard is removed and so introduce bugs / weird behavior with the launcher.

That's why when the app is on sdcard you'll see one warning tab in the application. The possibility to move to sdcard is allowed by the app just so that you can have choice in case you don't care loosing some feature/reliability of the app and wants to minimize the the storage used by the app. Another thing you might be interested : if you are trying to minimize size used by the app is that version of the app that only target the CPU arch of your phone. You can download it from : http://nightlies.csipsimple.com/stable/1.00.00/ for nexus S choose the "armeabi-v7a".
