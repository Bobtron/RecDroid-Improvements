csipsimple - issue #2663

Using speakerphone breaks app until I restart phone

Posted on Mar 9, 2014 by Swift Monkey

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Dial any number 2. Place the call on speaker phone 3.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The call to be routed through the speaker phone, instead I get a distorted noise. You can hear the noise by following the link, though it doesn't quite capture how scratchy it sounds and please turn down your volume before playing: http://vocaroo.com/i/s1oWqIpPcQwz. Voice to/from the device fails after this even after trying to exit speakerphone.

If I am able to hang up the call (50%~), CSipSimple still thinks its connected to my PBX but my PBX reports the phone is unreachable. If I try to make another call, the number disappears after I press dial and nothing happens. It won't work again until I restart the phone.

Typically, I am unable to end the call, clicking end does abosulutly nothing after hearing the noise and I must restart my phone to stop the call/application. If I don't restart the phone either way, CSipSimple drains my battery very quickly.

What version of the product are you using? On what device / operating system? Samsung Galaxy S4 running CyanogenMod 11 Android 4.4.2 cSipSimple: 1.02.00 r2330

Please provide any additional information below. My phone worked great with cSipSimple for a while, then all of a sudden I stopped being able to properly send/receive calls. I'm not sure if it was an update to my phone or the app that caused the issue.

I've since resetting the phone to factory defaults and duplicated the settings from my girlfriends Samsung Note 2, also running CM11, which has no issues making calls but still no luck.

I've tried GSM, G729, ULAW and ALAW.

I've also tried enabling the galaxy audio fix, which I believed stopped all sound working correctly.
