csipsimple - issue #940

Filters backup problems

Posted on May 9, 2011 by Happy Panda

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.Set either a second (or third, forth,..) filter rule to any of your SIP accounts, or, in case you have Skype installed, just the first one to it. 2. Backup your accounts. 3. Restore that backup. Those newly created filter rules have been lost.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Filter rules should all be backep up.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.02-00 r839 nightly build, on a Motorola Defy phone running the UK 3.4.3-11 ROM (Froyo 2.2.1).

Please provide any additional information below. Problem showed in all previous versions of CSipSimple.
