Posted on Mar 12, 2013 by Quick Hippo

Video is experimental. If you want to debug it on non supported yet features/platforms, you are on your own. I can help on the csipsimple-dev group but I've not enough free time to address all guys that are doing bleeding edge stuff. I will also need actual traces because you, as a developer, obviously know it's impossible to debug something you don't see. The "it doesn't work" only is not enough to understand/fix something. So please, join de csipsimple-dev group and post/discuss there. Also be aware video is not yet ready to be shipped to user and I strongly discourage people to redistribute it as a product as would just give a very wrong message to users. I want that to be an alpha feature for now as it's clearly not ready for users. When it will land in user hands I want them to have some real product and not a beta stuff and be discouraged to use csipsimple because it's not good on this feature.
