csipsimple - issue #926

Add an option to base remote side compution on DisplayName instead of userName to address callback uses cases

Posted on May 5, 2011 by Massive Elephant

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Create sipgate account on csipsimple and login 2. Configure my GV gggggggggg to ring only my sipgate number ssssssssss 3. Call my GV number from other phone pppppppppp

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? expected: csipsimple rings and displays pppppppppp as caller ID (and displays photo of pppppppppp what i see: csipsimple rings and displays ssssssssss pppppppppp (in much smaller font)

plus photo of ssssssssss

In other words, csipsimple always shows my own sipgate number as the primary caller id instead of the identity of the original caller (pppppppppp)

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Nexus One, CM Froyo, latest dev release

Please provide any additional information below.

This might not be a csipsimple issue. It may be a google voice issue. Not sure.

Comment #1

Posted on May 5, 2011 by Quick Hippo

I think that's link to the fact Google Voice callback using sssssss as user name and ppppppp as "display name".

CSipSimple considers that the username is the number to take into account to learn about what is actually calling you. And it's not wrong... The line that is calling you is not your remote side, but actually your own phone number. Then it calls the other side, but the actual phone number that is calling you (your sipgate SIP account) is sssssssss. It's a callback ;).

So I don't think that's a bug in csipsimple.

However, I understand your need. That's a specific case. I think that in order to address this case, I could add an extra setting (not enabled by default and only available in expert mode) to take displayName instead of userName to compute the remote side number. It would solve this use case without breaking everything for others.
