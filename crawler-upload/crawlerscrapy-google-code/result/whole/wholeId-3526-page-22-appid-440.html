csipsimple - issue #1291

Ongoing calls go on hold because the Screen does not lock properly on Motorola Milestone/Droid

Posted on Sep 25, 2011 by Swift Rabbit

What steps will reproduce the problem?

  1. Place/Receive SIP call
  2. Put handset on ear... after a while...
  3. Call goes ON HOLD

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Locked screen

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? Latest release. Android 2.2.1

Please provide any additional information below. Earlier CSipSimple versions managed this much better. Now I am not able to give serious use to CSipSimple since every call goes on hold in some moment, and I don't find anywhere in the UI how to fix this. I am using commercial Bria 1.1.6 that does not have this issue, but I'd like to get back to CSipSimple.
