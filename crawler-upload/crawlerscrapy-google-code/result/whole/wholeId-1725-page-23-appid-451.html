freewifi-connect - issue #14

Authentication fails on Android Jelly Bean (4.1) - Nexus S

Posted on Aug 16, 2012 by Swift Monkey

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Install FreeWifi Connect on an Android 4.1 terminal 2. Setup your FreeWifi login and password 3. Try to connect to a FreeWifi network

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The phone should properly authenticate to the FreeWifi network. Instead, the authentication fails.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? A Google Nexus S phone, not rooted, and running the stock 4.1 ROM

Please provide any additional information below. This was working on the same phone until it got updated to android 4.1
