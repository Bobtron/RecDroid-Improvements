viperrom - issue #78

Official LoS Issue for Samsung Epic 4G Touch - Please post logs!

Posted on Nov 1, 2011 by Grumpy Dog

Ok everyone, I would like to keep track of everything I can find on this LoS issue. I am going to repost some info from my LoS Checker thread (http://bit.ly/LoS_XDA)

To help find these issues, follow these directions on a phone that is currently LoS or Ghost LoS and when you post in here, please specify if you are LoS or Ghost LoS and what version of viperMOD LoS Checker you are running, if any...

type "su" (without the quotes, and not necessary if you're in adb)

then type:

service call connectivity 4 > /sdcard/log1.log

service call connectivity 5 > /sdcard/log2.log

getprop | grep -i ril > /sdcard/log3.log

Information:

Right now, it seems that in log1.log, you may see "Data Disabled" (http://pastebin.com/K0AxZzjQ) or "Suspended" (http://pastebin.com/E5tmCiWz) when you are experiencing LoS.

log2.log is just log1.log with more information about all of the phone's radios. log1.log is just condensed with all the information I usually need.

log3.log gets information about the phone radio. It seems that if you see "Suspeneded" in log1.log you are likely to see the line "[ril.prl_ver_1]" read "[P:default ]". I need confirmation of this to be sure. You may see PRL read default but not see data show "Suspended" or vice versa.

Please post these logs as attachments or links to pastebin. Please don't just paste the log as a response.
