csipsimple - issue #1367

Gingerbread dialer integration

Posted on Nov 7, 2011 by Swift Rhino

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Install CSIPSimple on Gingerbread 2. Setup your account and enable settings for dialer integration 3. Go to home screen 4. Open android dialer app. Select a contact or recently dialed number 5. Hit dial.

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Expected output: Prompt to use Csipsimple or a normal outgoing voice call. Observed output: Android does not give you an option at all, and instead completes it via normal outgoing call.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? 0.02-03 r944 from the Market OS: Gingerbread 2.3.5 Hardware: HTC HD2 ROM: MIUI

Please provide any additional information below.
