Comment #6

Posted on Oct 25, 2010 by Quick Hippo

Fix available BUT -- read carefully that : For guys who want to test the fix (in fact, I'd say the workaround); * Install the 0.00-15-05 * Go in settings. Here press menu and choose "Expert mode" * Enter Media section and scroll to "Use software volume". Tick the check box to enable the option. * Back & Back to go the dialer... Enjoy ;).

When you'll press the volume button you'll see the global amplification settings from the software instead of seeing the unworking android volume controller. Be really careful cause on your phone, the speaker already have a big gain. So I advise you to keep the value as lower as possible to not produce echo on the remote contact side.

Like for the android volume popup, this popup will vanish after 5 second when controlled from the volume key. But ... there is a way to show this popup using Menu (while on the in call screen) and "Media" (and it will not quit until you say "Ok" or press back button once). You can also adjust micro control here and also enable or disable echo cancellation (but this echo cancellation is not designed by pjsip guys to cancel sound if it's too loud from your rear-speaker or earpiece and is recorder back from the mic).

Let me know how it goes. Also interested about voice quality with this version (as said in previous comment).
