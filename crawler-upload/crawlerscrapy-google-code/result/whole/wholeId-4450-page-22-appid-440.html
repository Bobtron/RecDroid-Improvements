csipsimple - issue #402

Proximity sensor switches off audio stream

Posted on Nov 21, 2010 by Helpful Wombat

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1.make a call 2.activate the proximity sensor (by placing the phone to your ear

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? The screen switches off and the touchscreenis disabled, but the audio cuts off as well. It is resumed when the phone is held on a distance, when the screen lights as well

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? dell streak 2.2

Please provide any additional information below. Suggest to provide a "enable proximity sensor" tickmark under "user interface" tab

Comment #1

Posted on Nov 21, 2010 by Quick Hippo

There is already an option for that: In settings press menu and switch to expert mode. Then go in user interface section and enable keep awake while in call. It is an option that is by default enabled on htc devices cause they have a bug : psp mode bug. It only affect wifi connection on htc so the option only affect behaviour on wifi. Let me know if it helps on steack so that I do something to automatically turn the setting on for Dell too.

With "with enable keep awake while in call" the audio works like a charm! When the proxi sensor activates the display shows a "lock" icon but isn't switched off, and audio keeps being good.
