csipsimple - issue #259

Initial settings for Samsung Galaxy S

Posted on Oct 2, 2010 by Helpful Giraffe

Running latest CSipSimple (13-02) works really well with my Samsung Galaxy S on Firmware JPK (Froyo 2.2) after tweaking some settings in audio:

Mikrofonpegel: 0.4 Lautsprecherpegel: 0.2

(don't know english wording for this, sorry)

With these two settings, the person speaking into the galaxy s has a sharp voice and quality is good on the other side, and the volume of the other person is still very loud, but not loud enough for to produce echoes in combination with 0.4 Mikrofonpegel.

These should be reasonable default values for a Galaxy S. And when https://code.google.com/p/csipsimple/issues/detail?id=254 is fixed, this would be the first sip-app to be really usable on a galaxy s! Nice!
