csipsimple - issue #2062

Pause (,) and Wait (;) in DTMF ignored in intergration of devices without GSM application

Posted on Nov 7, 2012 by Happy Kangaroo

What steps will reproduce the problem?

  1. store a dialstring like +4237931396,12345 in address book
  2. select contact in the addressbook or use 3rd party app dialing such a number
  3. select a csipsimple connected voip account or even GSM (if available) to be used

What is the expected output? What do you see instead?

Just dial the number, process the "pause (,)" and/or "wait (;)", and finally send the DTMF digits.

In the account selection pop-up you'll find a "modified" dialstring where Pause (,) and Wait (;) are removed from the dialstring. This leads to an invalid number, eg.: +4237931396,12345 mutates to +423793139612345 and leads to "Number you have dialed is not assigned"

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system?

CSipSimple 0.04-04r1916 Samsung GT-P7500 Android 4.0.4

Please provide any additional information below.

pls see screenshot attached to CSipSimple Error-Log reports

Comment #3

Posted on Dec 3, 2012 by Quick Hippo

No p characters are not supported because could actually be part of the SIP uri. (keep in mind that SIP works with sip addresses, not with numbers necessarily). So interpreting "p" character would break for any actual user name containing "p" The only workaround is to replace your numbers with "," or ";" in your contact book.
