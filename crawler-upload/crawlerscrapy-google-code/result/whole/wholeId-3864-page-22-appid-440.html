csipsimple - issue #970

STUN Server with ICE fails to have incoming audio on NAT(Bell 3G)

Posted on May 18, 2011 by Happy Camel

What steps will reproduce the problem? 1. Add FreePhoneLine.ca to accounts 2. Enable STUN and ICE 3. Make or receive call on 3G(works fine on Wifi)

What is the expected output? What do you see instead? Two way audio is expected, instead I am able to transmit audio out but not receive audio.

What version of the product are you using? On what operating system? The latest Csipsimple from the market, Android OS 2.2

Please provide any additional information below. STUN settings appear to work fine on Linphone(Using now) and 3CXPhone, though neither are as polished as Csipsimple. Tried multiple STUN servers so it appears as though it is isolated to Csipsimple(and SipDroid for that matter).
