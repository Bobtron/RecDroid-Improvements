Expected behavior

I expected that using this app I will able to add music to the playlist from "ARTIST" tabs folder.

Actual behavior

When I try to add songs of Artist's folder to the playlist, the app crashes after a moment.

How to reproduce

  • Download and install the latest v2.0.5-beta2 from here
  • Open the app and go to ARTIST tab.
  • You will see many folders, tap and open a folder (as your choice).
  • Then tap "three dots" button on the top.
  • Then press "Add to..." and after that tap on "Playlist".
  • Select a playlist where you want to add your songs and then notice the crash.
  • App version: 2.0.5 beta2 free
  • Operating system: Android 6.0.1
  • Device: Samsung Galaxy A5 (2016)
