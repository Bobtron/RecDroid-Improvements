Description of bug:

A large number of artists are missing from the artist tab, and instead are all stored under one artist's name.What those songs have in common is they have no album name, and therefore are given the default album name "music". This is identical to issue #52 which is now closed, but the problem still exists. Looking at issue #52 should recap exactly what the problem is.

Steps to reproduce:

  1. Store music in "music" folder without an album name
  2. Open the app and go to artist or album tab

Expected outcome:

Songs will be listed under their given artist as per their ID3 artist tag.

Observations/Actual Result:

In the artist tab, songs without an album will be stored under the same artist or ""
