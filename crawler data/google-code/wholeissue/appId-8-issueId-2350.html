ale5000-git Sep 29, 2016

Contributor

With the normal Xposed you will probably never pass SafetyNet.
To have signature spoofing without Xposed you can use Tingle or Needle.

There are 2 possibilities:

  1. Use Open GApps, and after installing them remove root and Xposed. In this case it is possible (I cannot guarantee) that you will pass SafetyNet.
  2. Wait for the Microg droidguard helper to become compatible with KitKat, but after that you still have to remove Xposed (or using systemless Xposed that do not work in your case) to pass SafetyNet.
