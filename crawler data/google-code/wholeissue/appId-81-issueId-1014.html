Thanks for nice wallet!

But I have problem to explain my friend to use this wallet for incoming transactions from me. I understand the LN protocol and understand your idea: for incoming he should made some outcoming payments. It's because he creates channel with some random nodes and these random nodes will not put initiated bitcoins to his side. But it's very ugly idea: for getting of payments he should make payments before. Your wallet will not be widely used by people if they want to get payments from some people.

Question: in future will be there possible to make feature "to be as node"?

For example: I want to pay my friend for something. He installs your wallet, he doesn't have bitcoins. After he shows to me QR code of his node (BLW) and I create channel with him directly. I create channel so I make funding deposit. And after confirmations I can send to him micro bitcoins. Do you have plans about this? May be there should be supporting from Olympus server. But I think this feature should be if you position your wallet as wallet for incoming payments.
