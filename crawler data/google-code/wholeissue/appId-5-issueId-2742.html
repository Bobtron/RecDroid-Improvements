The big change with this launcher is the new Google Search UI. Rather than a pill-shaped widget sticking out of the side of the display, this new Pixel Launcher has a full-width gray bar that’s sitting between the docked apps and the on-screen navigation buttons. And managed to capture it in a couple of brief videos:

Can you add it to Lawnchair in the future?
