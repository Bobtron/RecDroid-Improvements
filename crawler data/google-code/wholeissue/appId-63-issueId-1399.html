My Mail for future cases:

MemeCreator - License violation - Google Play

Hi there,

I were made aware of your MemeCreator on Google Play (),
which is a copy of the App MemeTastic (https://github.com/gsantner/memetastic),
developed by me and contributors, licensed GPLv3.

You are not allowed to publish the app without handing out
source code/changes to build the app. (You added Ads and removed neccessary credits,
About, Info,..). Also you need to keep and show required copyright notices.
You can obtain a copy of the GPLv3 license here: https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.en.html

You MUST either hand out the code and show copyright notices, or remove the app.
For more details see https://github.com/gsantner/memetastic#licensing .

I'm awaiting a answer soon.

Takedown notice text:

A simple MemeCreator for Android. It is an GPLv3 licensed app, developed by me and contributors on GitHub.
It features a simple and ad-free experience for creating memes by image templates, camera & gallery pictures.
The infringing apps don't show or removed copyright notices (including mine those of contributors, of used libraries), do not tell the used license (GPLv3), and do not hand out the source code (or the changes) as required by the license, so they violate the terms of the app's license. Some didn't even change app name or icon, or just copied the description too.

Infrigning: All apps are a copy of MemeTastic violating and not complying with the terms of the project's license. Please remove these apps from Google Play Store.


Thee apps seem to use mostly code of MemeTastic from the codebase starting from v0.9.7, which code can be found here: https://github.com/gsantner/memetastic/tree/v0.9.7
