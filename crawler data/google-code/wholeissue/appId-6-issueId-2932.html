I'm going to break out of your template a bit. The info will be there, but it's more complex than what is expected vs result.

Shuttle version:

2.0.4-beta2 (Upgraded)

Device, OS:

HTC U11 US model. Android 8 with Magisk root and safety check valid (no Xposed or anything like that)

Description of bug:

There's a few parts to this.
I have a bunch of corrupt files from an unrelated tragedy and less than ideal recovery. Shuttle conveniently detects the files and lists them in Unknown Artist with 0:00 length.
I have a lot of Japanese music, so some of these files have Unicode filenames, though it doesn't seem to be correlated to the issues.

Upon selecting and deleting files, some delete just fine. Out of 300, about 75 deleted with only a force close. The MediaStore and listings updated, the files were deleted.
Most of them are being spoilsports.

  • I select a group of files (due to the issues below, they need to be in the same folder).
  • I hit delete.
  • It thinks for a bit (more than 3s seems like a while to wait for 6 files).
  • It then asks for Storage Permission, which I've provided.
  • In order for the Deletion to succeed (it always says it fails), I need to go to the folder where the files are located in the permission dialog and select it. This is annoying, of course, as proper procedure is to sort your music into folders.
  • Assuming I do this correctly, the files are now gone, but the MediaStore isn't updated, so I need to manually trigger an update. The listing is also corrupted, so I need to kill it anyway while I scan the MediaStore.
  • Repeat for every folder.....

Expected:

  • I hit delete.
  • The files are deleted promptly without asking for fake Permission. A progress would be ideal if you have many files selected.
  • It says succeeded if it works
  • It updates the MediaStore
  • It doesn't force close or corrupt the listing.
